# Employee HR Analytics Dashboard 📊

An interactive Power BI Dashboard developed to analyze workforce demographics, salary structures, and department-level insights for a company of 5,000 employees.

## 📌 Key Insights & Features
* **Overview Dashboard:** Tracks high-level KPIs like Total Headcount (5,000 employees), Total Payroll Cost, Average Salary, and Average Employee Age/Experience.
* **Demographics Analysis:** Visualizes workforce diversity (Gender distribution), Marital Status, and Geographical spread (City-wise data) to support Diversity & Inclusion (D&I) initiatives.
* **Departmental & Position Deep Dive:** Breaks down salary allocation and employee count by departments (Sales, HR, IT, Finance) and specific job positions to aid in workforce budget planning.
