# 📊 Employee HR Analytics Dashboard

An interactive, end-to-end Power BI Dashboard designed to analyze workforce demographics, compensation structures, and departmental performance metrics for a robust organization of **5,000 employees**. 

This project transforms raw HR transactional data into actionable organizational insights to assist HR leaders and C-suite executives in making data-driven decisions around payroll optimization, diversity initiatives, and talent retention.

---

## 🚀 Key Business Insights & Analytical Breakdown

### 📂 1. Executive Workforce Overview (Page 1)
This dashboard offers a bird's-eye view of the organization's current size, maturity, and financial scale.
* **Workforce Scale:** Management of **5,000 active employees** across multiple business segments.
* **Financial Commitment:** Tracks a substantial annual baseline salary footprint of **₹46.93 Crores (469.3M)**, breaking down components like Gross Salary, HRA, DA, and PF.
* **Compensation Benchmarking:** Highlights an average organizational salary of **₹93,861**, setting a baseline for market competitiveness.
* **Workforce Maturity & Tenure:** The organization reflects a mature workforce with an **Average Age of 40.4 years**, backed by a high **Average Total Experience of 19.4 years** and an **Average Company Tenure of 9.8 years**, indicating strong organizational stability.

### 👥 2. Demographics & Workforce Diversity (Page 2)
Focuses on tracking Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion (DE&I) along with geographic presence.
* **Perfect Gender Equilibrium:** The dataset reveals an exceptionally balanced distribution across the workforce:
  * **Female:** 1,674 Employees
  * **Other:** 1,668 Employees
  * **Male:** 1,658 Employees
  * *Strategic Impact:* Enables HR to demonstrate compliance with modern corporate diversity goals and balanced recruitment pipelines.
* **Geographical Distribution:** Identifies primary talent hubs and regional hubs. **Delhi (1,322 employees)** and **Mumbai (847 employees)** dominate the talent pool, followed by critical regional operations in **Ranchi (208)**, **Hyderabad (175)**, and **Bangalore (172)**.
* **Social Profile Matrix:** Evaluates Marital Status metrics to help HR tailor corporate wellness benefits, health insurance policies, and family-centric engagement programs.

### 🏢 3. Departmental Structures & Position Insights (Page 3)
A granular deep-dive into internal resource allocation, operational costs, and structural hierarchy.
* **Headcount by Function:** Analyzes departmental size across all 5 operational wings:
  * **Human Resources:** 1,035 employees *(Highest headcount)*
  * **IT:** 1,005 employees
  * **Sales:** 1,004 employees
  * **Finance:** 989 employees
  * **Marketing:** 967 employees
* **Cost Center Analysis:** Cross-references headcount against gross payroll budgets to pinpoint which departments or technical roles (e.g., Sales Directors vs. HR Executives) act as primary cost drivers.
* **Tenure vs. Hierarchy:** Connects 'In-Company Years' with job positions to spot promotion bottlenecks, talent gaps, and retention trends before they impact operational performance.

---

## 💡 Strategic Decisions Driven by This Dashboard

1. **Financial Optimization:** Helps executive management assess if payroll costs are aligned with productivity across departments (e.g., evaluating IT or Sales resource load).
2. **Targeted Recruitment & Expansion:** With talent highly concentrated in Delhi and Mumbai, management can make informed choices regarding future physical office expansions or focusing on remote-first hiring in growing hubs like Bangalore and Hyderabad.
3. **Retention & Succession Planning:** By monitoring the high average experience (19.4 years), HR can actively model future retirement pipelines and implement robust corporate mentoring or succession plans.

## 🛠️ Technical Toolkit & Methodology

* **Data Profiling & Source Layer:** Structured and validated over Microsoft Excel.
* **Data Modeling & Architecture:** Built star-schema style relationships handling structural calculations inside Power BI.
* **DAX Formulas (Data Analysis Expressions):** Implemented custom measures for dynamic cost tracking, payroll aggregation, and average tenure indicators.
* **UI/UX Design UI Canvas:** Styled with a clean, light theme background to keep focus purely on core business visualizations (Bar charts, Slicers, Donut charts, and KPI cards).
